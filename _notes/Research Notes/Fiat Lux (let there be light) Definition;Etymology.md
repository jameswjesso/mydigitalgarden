## Fiat Lux (let there be light) Definition

Created: 2022-12-05

*****

Fiat Lux: let there be light.

...

Lux meaning light
fiat coming from *facio*, meaning "to do" or "to make"

The phrase comes from from the Biblical book of Genesis 1:3
